Child Safety Standards

Last updated: June 7, 2026 · Effective: June 7, 2026

⚠️ Zero tolerance: Mapsgram has a zero-tolerance policy for content or behavior involving the sexual abuse, exploitation, or endangerment of minors. We act swiftly to remove such content and report it to the appropriate authorities.

1. Minimum Age and Eligibility

Mapsgram is intended for users aged 13 and older. Accounts identified as belonging to users under 13 are immediately suspended and permanently deleted. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at kadir.yetis@mapsgram.com.

2. Prevention of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Mapsgram strictly prohibits, and takes proactive measures against, the following:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) in any form (photos, videos, text)
  • Grooming behavior or attempts to develop inappropriate relationships with minors
  • Sharing, soliciting, or trading of sexually explicit content involving minors
  • Sextortion targeting minors
  • Sexualized content involving fictional minors
  • Any other behavior that may endanger or exploit children

How We Detect and Prevent CSAE

  • Content moderation: User-generated content (stories, photos, messages) is reviewed when reported or flagged by automated systems.
  • User reporting tools: In-app reporting available on every story, profile, and direct message. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.
  • Account suspension: Accounts violating these standards are immediately suspended pending review, then permanently banned upon confirmation.
  • IP and device blocking: Repeat offenders are blocked at the device level to prevent re-registration.
  • Law enforcement cooperation: We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations and report CSAM to relevant authorities (in Türkiye: İhbarweb; internationally: NCMEC where applicable).

3. In-App Reporting

Users can report concerns about child safety through the app:

  • Report a story: Open any story → tap the "..." menu → Report → Select "Inappropriate / Child safety"
  • Report a user: Open any profile → tap the "..." menu → Report → Select "Inappropriate / Child safety"
  • Report a direct message: Long-press any message → Report → Select reason

All child safety reports are prioritized and reviewed by our safety team within 24 hours.

4. Compliance with Legal Standards

Mapsgram complies with all applicable laws relating to the protection of minors, including but not limited to:

  • Türkiye: Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK, Law No. 6698) and Internet Crimes Law (Law No. 5651)
  • European Union: General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR — EU 2016/679), Digital Services Act (DSA)
  • United States: Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA — for U.S. users where applicable)
  • International: United Nations Convention on the Rights of the Child

5. Reporting to Authorities

When Mapsgram identifies or receives a verified report of CSAM:

  • The content is immediately removed
  • The account is permanently banned
  • We report the incident to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable
  • For users in Türkiye, we report to the Information and Communication Technologies Authority (BTK) and law enforcement as required
  • We preserve all evidence in accordance with applicable legal requirements

6. Designated Point of Contact

Our designated point of contact for child safety concerns is responsible and trained to respond to inquiries about our CSAM prevention practices and compliance with this policy.

  • Email: kadir.yetis@mapsgram.com
  • Subject line: "Child Safety — [your concern]"
  • Response time: Within 24 hours for urgent matters

7. Privacy by Design

Mapsgram is designed with privacy in mind. We have committed to never build certain features that could endanger users — including minors:

  • No profile visitor lists (protects visitor privacy)
  • No friend radar history (location is sensitive data)
  • No anonymous story viewing (story owners can see who viewed)
  • No hidden read receipts (transparent communication)
  • No DM screenshot blocking (preserves evidence integrity)

For full privacy commitments, see our Privacy Policy.

8. Continuous Improvement

This policy is reviewed and updated regularly. We continuously improve our detection systems, reporting tools, and moderation processes based on evolving threats and best practices.

9. Resources

If you or someone you know needs help:

  • Türkiye — İhbarweb: https://ihbarweb.org.tr
  • International — NCMEC CyberTipline: https://report.cybertip.org
  • INHOPE (international network): https://www.inhope.org
