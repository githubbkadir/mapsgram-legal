Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026 · Effective: June 4, 2026

🛡️ Our Privacy Commitment — Things We Will Never Build

Even for a premium subscription or any payment, we will never offer the following features on Mapsgram. These features either violate user privacy or expose the data of users and non-user third parties to unjustifiable risk:

FeatureWhy we won't build it
Profile visitor listViolates the visitor's own privacy rights (KVKK Art. 4 / GDPR Art. 5)
Friend radar history ("who passed near me")Exposes others' location history (KVKK Art. 6 sensitive data / GDPR special category)
Demographics under 5 usersViolates k-anonymity; small samples can re-identify individuals
Hiding DM read receiptsA feature designed to deceive the other party
DM screenshot warnings / blockersCompromises evidence integrity in judicial processes
Anonymous story viewing (stealth)Violates the story owner's right to know "who viewed?", enables stalker culture

This list only grows; we never shrink it. Privacy review is performed before adding new features, and these items will not be added even at the premium tier.

1. Data Controller

This Privacy Policy describes how Mapsgram ("App", "Service") processes your personal data.

  • Data controller: Mapsgram
  • Legal contact: kadir.yetis@mapsgram.com (postal address provided upon request)
  • Email: kadir.yetis@mapsgram.com
  • Web: https://mapsgram.com

Mapsgram processes data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR — 2016/679, EU) and the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK — Law No. 6698).

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account information (Google Sign-In)

  • Email address
  • Name (from your Google profile)
  • Profile photo (Google avatar URL)

2.2 Profile information (you provide)

  • Username (@username)
  • Display name
  • Bio (optional)
  • Age and gender (optional, for demographic aggregation)
  • Secret word (stored as hash)
  • Avatar

2.3 Location data

  • Precise GPS location (only while the app is in the foreground — no background location collection)
  • Story verification (to confirm you are at the venue)
  • Friend proximity notifications
  • Venue recommendations
  • Demographic aggregation (anonymized)
  • Old location records are automatically deleted after 24 hours

2.4 Content

  • Story photos and videos (Cloudflare R2)
  • DM messages, photo attachments
  • Check-in records
  • Story reactions (emoji), comments, view records

2.5 Friendship information

  • Friendship requests (sent + received)
  • Accepted friends list
  • Block list

2.6 Device information

  • Push notification token (Expo Push)
  • App session info
  • Crash logs (for technical debugging)

4. Data Sharing (Third Parties)

We share data with the following service providers:

  • Cloudflare R2 (photo/video storage, EU/US)
  • Railway (application server, US)
  • Google (OAuth, Maps SDK, Places API)
  • Ticketmaster (receives event data, no user data is shared)
  • Expo Push (push notifications)

We do not sell your data to ad networks and do not share with marketers. The "Sponsored Venues" feature provides venue owners with anonymous aggregate statistics, not your personal data.

6. Data Subject Rights (GDPR Art. 15-22 + KVKK Art. 11)

  • Right to know: Learn which data is being processed
  • Right to access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate/incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: From within the app: Settings → Danger Zone → Delete My Account, or via this page
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, email kadir.yetis@mapsgram.com. We respond within 30 days.

Under GDPR you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Under KVKK you may file a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Authority of Türkiye.

7. Children's Data

Mapsgram is not designed for users under 13. Accounts identified as belonging to users under 13 are deleted immediately.

8. Security

  • All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS
  • Passwords are hashed (bcrypt + salt)
  • JWT tokens valid for 7 days
  • Database access is restricted to authorized personnel

In case of a data breach, we notify users and authorities within 72 hours as required by GDPR and KVKK.

9. International Data Transfer

Some of our service providers are located outside the EU: Google (US), Railway (US), Cloudflare (Global). These transfers are safeguarded by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) or equivalent mechanisms.

10. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The mobile app does not use traditional web cookies. Data stored on your device:

  • JWT session token (AsyncStorage)
  • User preferences
  • Notification history

This data stays only on your device.

11. Policy Changes

For significant changes, we will send an in-app notification and update the "Last updated" date.
